Strawberry Yoghurt Popsicles

These delicious strawberry yoghurt popsicles are easy to make and perfect for the hot summer months.

You'll Need:

  • 1 lbs of fresh cut strawberries
  • 1/2 a lemon
  • 2 Tbsp of honey
  • 500 mls of plain greek yoghurt
  • 2 Tbsp of honey
  • A couple drops of vanilla
  • Popsicle molds

Tip!!!

If you don't have fresh fruit, use frozen and microwave until softer and jucier! (It's cheaper and the popsicles are still delicious!)

Directions

  1. Blend strawberries, lemon juice, and honey in a blender until smooth.
  2. In separate bowl, mix the yoghurt, honey, and vanilla.
  3. Alternating the strawberry and yoghurt mixtures, pour into popsicle molds.
  4. Freeze 4 hours or more before serving.

Enjoy!
